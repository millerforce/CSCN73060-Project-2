SocketBuffer PacketHandler::serialize ( Packet & packet)
static

Serializes a Packet into a fixed-size buffer.

The packet fields are written sequentially into the buffer:

  • UUID (16 bytes)
  • Timestamp (int)
  • Fuel (float)
  • CRC (uint16_t)

The CRC is calculated over all preceding bytes and then appended.

Parameters
packetThe packet to serialize. Its CRC field will be updated.
Returns
SocketBuffer The serialized packet buffer.
